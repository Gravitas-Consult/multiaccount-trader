HOW DOES IT WORK

 

MultiAccount Trader is a 100% cloud-based copier system, designed to allow you the management of multiple trade accounts simultaneously, independent of the Broker(s) you are using, in an ultra-low latency environment. Be sure that all high-frequency trading strategies are welcome!

Simply speaking, your trade accounts will be logged in into our cloud based multiaccount trader software. You choose the risk measurements and capital allocation methods (no need for you to use an expensive VPS or overload your computer with platforms). After that, every order you give on your Master Account -independent of the platform you are using or your physical distance to your broker’s data center - will be replicated in a few milliseconds.

 

MAT Explained

 
  1. It's simple: you have a MASTER ACCOUNT AND A FOLLOWER ACCOUNT ( Or many follower accounts if you wish). YOU CONTROL THE TRADES ON YOUR MASTER ACCOUNT; the trades get replicated on the follower accounts.
  2. After setting up your favorite allocation method,(in this example an equity ratio of 0.8) you are READY to start trading in your MASTER and FOLLOWER accounts simultaneously.
  3. The buy order for 1 Lot EURUSD is done on the MASTER account, you see in below screenshot, that the trade is replicated perfectly on the FOLLOWER account. The opening price is exactly the same, the trades opened at the same second as you can see, and the volume was adjusted for the equity ratio 0.8, even with the accounts being on DIFFERENT BASE CURRENCIES.
  4. By the same token, you can close the Trade on the MASTER ACCOUNT. It gets closed the same time on the FOLLOWER: You can see below. both trades closed at the same time at the same price.
  5. VOILA! TWO ACCOUNTS TRADED PERFECTLY SIMULTANEOUSLY.*

* For various reasons such as different account types, brokers or execution slippage, even with MAT ultra low latency, there is a chance the opening and close prices are not exactly the same.

Why MultiAccount and not a Copier EA?

 
Much Lower Latency
Even if installed on a low latency VPS, most Copier EA’s end up presenting a >1sec latency due to the communication time between the platform running the EA and the EA’s main receiver server, and due to delays on execution for overstretched VPS memory usage. With our MultiAccount Trader, both these issues are solved!
No need for VPS or Multiple Platforms
For effectively running a Copier EA you need to invest in a lot of things which will be expensive for you: subscribing to 1 or several costly VPS services, plus guaranteeing they are always updated, safe and well serviced. The large majority of traders end up not doing this, which might translate into enormously expensive issues. With MultiAccount Trader, all your needs and requirements are included so you don’t need to worry about anything.
 
 
 
No need for Multiple Platforms running
It can become a nightmare to control dozens of running MT4 platforms at the same time. Especially if you run all of them with individual Copier EA’s as well. By doing so, you induce yourself into repetitive work of EA parametrization and account control, opening doors to human errors that can end up being fatal for your trading. With MultiAccount Trader, all that workload is automatized. You can fully focus on what matters: your trading.
Avoid EA parameter issues
All Copier EA’s need to be parametrized after installation, directly on the EA page at your MT4. As such, in case any issue happens with your EA parametrization either through Live Update errors or other unforeseeable scenario, the consequences can be dire. By using our services, you protect yourself from these issues.
 
 
 
No orphan trades left behind
While using a Copier EA, you are under the risk of leaving orphan trades in 1 or several of your Follower accounts, after closing the respective Master trade. This might happen due to several factors, such but not limited to connection failure on the platforms, issue with EA parametrization or invalid pricing. Our innovative Trade ID system avoids these situations so no trades will be left behind.
Full flexibility for your strategy
Most Copier EA’s limit your ability to use several Master Accounts at the same time and/or the way capital allocation on your Follower Accounts will be done regarding the signals received by the Master Accounts. With MultiAccount Trader, you can build your replication structure 100% accordingly to your effective needs. Truly individualised to your specific needs.
